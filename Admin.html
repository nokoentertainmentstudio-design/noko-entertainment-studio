How Files Work
FILES

Images and videos are saved to the uploads/ folder inside your Testing Site directory.

VIDEO

MP4 only — max 50 MB. Compress with HandBrake or Adobe Media Encoder before uploading.

IMAGES

PNG or JPEG — max 10 MB. Optimise with Squoosh or TinyPNG for fastest load times.

SERVER

Keep node server.js running in Terminal for the site to work. Restart after moving files.

Our Story

We Make Extraordinary
Things Happen

Noko Entertainment Studio is a dynamic multimedia powerhouse based in Pretoria, South Africa. We blend artistic excellence with technical mastery to produce world-class content across every platform and medium.

From cinematic weddings and high-impact commercials to immersive game worlds, stunning 3D animations, and powerful software — we don't just create content, we craft experiences that move people.

Founded on a passion for storytelling and innovation, we've established ourselves as leaders in educational entertainment, animation, and digital production — serving clients from local brands to national campaigns.
